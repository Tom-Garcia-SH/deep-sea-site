Welcome to the Deep Sea Catalog!

This website contains a curated selection of deep-sea creatures that captured my interest during my research. The information presented here comes from the Monterey Bay Aquarium and its non-profit research center, the Monterey Bay Aquarium Research Institute (MBARI), dedicated to advancing our understanding of the world's oceans.
